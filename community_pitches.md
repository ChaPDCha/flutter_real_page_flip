# Community Outreach Pitches: Real Page Flip Engine

## Reddit (r/FlutterDev) - Showcase Post
**Title**: [Showcase] Real Page Flip Engine: Physics-based paper fold effect with haptics & sound 📖

**Body**:
Hi everyone! I’ve been working on a page-flip engine that focuses on high-fidelity interaction rather than just simple animations. 

It models real-world paper friction, resistance, and dynamic shadows. 

**Key Features**:
- 🛠️ **Physics-based**: Real-time modeling of paper resistance.
- ⚡ **Performance**: Optimized clipping for stable 60/120 FPS.
- 🖐️ **Tactile**: Integrated haptics and sound effects.
- ♿ **Accessibility**: Full semantics support.

Check it out on GitHub: https://github.com/ChaPDCha/Real_page_flip
(Repo will be public soon!)

Would love to hear your thoughts on the physics feel!
