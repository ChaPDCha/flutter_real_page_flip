# Changelog

## 1.1.0

- **Initial High-Fidelity Release**: Re-engineered for production-ready performance.
- **Sensory Experience**: Added integrated, physics-synchronized sound effects (`page_flip.mp3`) and haptic feedback.
- **Improved Reliability**: Implemented robust layout constraint protection to prevent "unbounded" errors.
- **Bilingual Documentation**: Added comprehensive Korean and English READMEs with video demonstrations.
- **Dual Licensing**: Implemented a transition to a Dual License model (Free for non-commercial, Paid for commercial).
- **Customization**: Enhanced modularity, allowing developers to easily swap sound and haptic handlers.
